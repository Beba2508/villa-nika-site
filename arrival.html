Arrival & Departure Information

Hello and welcome to Noble Riviera Villa Nika! Below you'll find all essential information regarding your arrival, parking, check-in, and departure process.

Villa Location: Kolavići ulica 29, Veprinac

Check-in Time: from 16:00 (4 PM)
Your house will be ready after 16:00 on the start date of your reservation. If you would like to arrive earlier - please let us know and we will check our availability.
Our host will greet you and give you the keys/codes and show you around. If you haven't completed the online registration, please present your IDs to the host upon arrival.

Parking: You can park at one of the five parking spots in front of the house. To open the parking gate, use code 258# or the remote from the keybox.

Check-In and Check-Out:
Check-in: 16:00h (4 PM)
Check-out: 10:00h (10 AM)
Please inform us of your estimated times in advance.

Noise and Disturbance:
Respect our neighbors and observe quiet hours: 1 PM–5 PM and 10 PM–10 AM. No parties/events allowed.

Smoking: Not allowed inside. Use designated outdoor areas and dispose properly.

Pets: Not allowed on the property.

Occupancy: Only registered guests may stay. Max 6 guests total.

Cleanliness & Damages: Keep the villa clean. Report issues. Guests are liable for damages.

Security: Lock all doors/windows when leaving. Do not share access code.

Pool & Outdoor: Use responsibly. No glass by the pool. Kids must be supervised.

Kitchen Use: Clean after use. Avoid leaving out food.

Waste: Dispose of trash in designated bins.

Emergency Contact: Please contact the host immediately in case of emergency.

Check-out is at 10:00h (10 AM), unless agreed otherwise.

Before leaving, please:

  • Close and lock all windows
  • Log out of your apps on the TV
  • Load dirty dishes in the dishwasher
  • Take out garbage and recycling
  • Check for all belongings
  • Leave keys in the keybox

Kindly notify us once you depart so we can alert our cleaning team. Thank you!
